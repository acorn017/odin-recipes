Skillet Zucchini and Squash

Description

Yellow squash, zucchini, onions and red bell peppers are abundant in the summer, and they make a beautiful presentation in this easy-to-make side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 medium yellow squash, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • minced chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat butter and olive oil together in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion once butter is bubbly; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Add bell pepper and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in yellow squash and zucchini slices.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook, stirring, for about 3 minutes.
  7. Stir in minced garlic and cook, stirring for about 1 minutes.
  8. Continue stirring on medium-high heat until squash has desired texture, about 2-3 minutes.
  9. (Optional) Sprinkle with minced chives before serving.
