Air-Fried Eggs On-The-Go

Description

This simple recipe gives you a warm, protein-packed meal to kick-start your day, all while your coffe is brewing.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Salt and/or pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Lightly spray a ramekin dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Pour in beaten eggs.
  3. Set airfryer to 370*F (185*C) and place the ramekin into the air fryer basket.
  4. Air fry for 3 minutes.
  5. Stir egg mix and air fry again for 3 minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and season to taste.
